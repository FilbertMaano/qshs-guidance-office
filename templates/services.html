Services

College Application

Assists students in the college application process. The guidance office section provides resources and guidance to help students navigate the complex college application process. This may include assistance with filling out application forms, writing essays, and preparing for interviews.

Incident Rerport

Users can submit incident reports regarding safety and security within the campus or surrounding areas, which will be reviewed by the guidance office section for appropriate action to safeguard the community's well-being.

Dormitory

Provide support and guidance to students living in dormitories. This includes providing students with handbooks that outline the rules and regulations of the dormitory, as well as providing guidance and support for dormitories in managing their living environment.

Guidance Calendar

The website's guidance office section offers a calendar of events, including workshops, counseling sessions, and seminars, enabling users to sign up for relevant activities.
