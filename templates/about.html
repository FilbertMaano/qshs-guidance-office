QSHS Guidance Office

The Guidance Office is a department that offers support and assistance to students with their academic, personal, and social development. It is staffed by guidance counselors who provide a range of services, such as academic advising, career guidance, and personal counseling. The goal of a guidance office is to help students succeed academically, emotionally, and socially by offering resources and support to help them navigate the challenges of school and life. Additionally, guidance offices may also provide resources and support for parents and teachers to ensure that students receive the best possible education and support.
